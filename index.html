About Me

I am a Product Designer based in New York City. I was previously a photographer and my eye for visuals brought me into design. I live for creating state-of-the-art experiences and bringing extraordinary ideas to life. I appreciate challenges, because it gives me an opportunity to learn and expand my knowledge. My skill set spectrum ranges from design to coding, including HTML/CSS and jQuery. I believe the user experience is the key to a successful product, and I validate my ideas through testing and research.

I love photography. The feeling of being behind the lens is absolutely incredible, and through photography I able to portray my perspective to the people. Everyone is different, and I think it’s amazing how people can take different messages, and have different emotions from the same image. Besides photography I enjoy creating nifty cocktails, playing poker, and an avid sports fan.
