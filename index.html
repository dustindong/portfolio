Philosophy

"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."
- Leonardo da Vinci.

As a designer, I believe simplicity is the key to having good design. Simplicity makes it easier for the user to navigate through your product while still enjoying a beautiful experience. The more we complicate things, the more it tends to fail, is harder to use, and is less applicable. I love what I do and strive to learn something new everyday.
