Hi, I'm Dustin

I am a Product Designer who loved the User Experience that is based in New York City. I was previously a photographer and my eye for visuals brought me into design. I live for creating state-of-the-art experiences and bringing extraordinary ideas to life. I appreciate challenges, because it gives me an opportunity to learn and expand my knowledge. My skill set spectrum ranges from research, design and coding, including HTML/CSS and jQuery.

I am a big believer in Design Sprints. I follow Googles Design Sprint and implement it into every project that I can. I love that it encourages original thinking through experimentation and iteration. It's a great way to focus on ideas that would normally be rejected. It also reduces the cost of failure, and it helps validate ideas with users.
